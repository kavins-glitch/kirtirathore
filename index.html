About the Founder

India's Only Female
Men's Couturier

Hardworking and passionate, Kirti Rathore has built an unparalleled legacy in customised, tailor-made clothes for men. Her label — known simply as KIRTI RATHORE — is headquartered in the posh locality of C-Scheme, Jaipur, where a high-tech manufacturing unit with the latest machinery and an expert workforce bring her designs to life.

Kirti Rathore has built a flourishing career through innovative technique and excellent design skills forged through exclusive workmanship. She is the only female designer for men's wear in the Fashion Industry — having showcased her creations on national and international platforms with many prestigious awards and accolades to her name.

"Fashion is a form of ugliness so intolerable that we have to alter it every six months."

She works primarily with eco-friendly organic fabrics that can be worn in every season. Beyond fashion, Kirti Rathore is a dedicated social activist who believes in giving back to the society. Ever since she launched her creative collection, commerce has been growing progressively — with a signature of architectural shapes, idiosyncratic prints on luxurious fabrics (linen, silk with vibrant colours) creating garments that are rich, graceful, and sustainable.

Celebrity Clientele

For Vivek Oberoi, the studio has been managing his complete wardrobe for over five years. The house has also designed for Amitabh Bachchan, Prabhas, Shahrukh Khan, Salman Khan, Arbaaz Khan, Tiger Shroff, Kartik Aaryan, Arjun Kapoor, Boney Kapoor, Ajay Devgan, Nawazuddin Siddiqui, and many more of Bollywood's finest.

Mission & Vision

Shaping the Future
of Indian Fashion

KIRTI RATHORE School of Designing prepares students for careers in fashion design. Forward-thinking faculty, administration, and staff are committed to facilitating an understanding of human-centred design with grounding in liberal arts traditions, values, and critical inquiry.

The Design Institute of Kirti Rathore aspires to lead the field in design education, preparing innovative, environmentally and socially responsible citizens who positively impact the world.

The institute is entirely focused on imparting necessary entrepreneurial and confidence-building skills. The designed professional programs provide a gateway to the world of fashion.
